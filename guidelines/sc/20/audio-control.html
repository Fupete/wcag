Audio Control

A

If any audio on a Web page plays automatically for more than 3 seconds, at least one of the following is true:

Pause or Stop
A mechanism is available to pause or stop the audio.
Volume Control
A mechanism is available to control the audio volume, independently from any other audio output of the user agent and the overall system.

Since any content that does not meet this success criterion can interfere with a user's ability to use the whole page, all content on the Web page (whether or not it is used to meet other success criteria) must meet this success criterion. See Conformance Requirement 5: Non-Interference.

A user agent's ability to disable auto-playing media and/or mute a specific sound source (independently from any other audio output, e.g. a browser allowing to mute the audio playing in a specific tab) are acceptable mechanisms to stop or control the volume of auto-playing audio content. However, not all user agents may have this capability.
